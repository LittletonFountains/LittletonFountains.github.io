Our Project:

Problem:

The current water fountains at the Littleton Middle School are underused. This is because students have a perception of bacteria on the fountainhead and the water is discolored and warm.

Solution

We plan to solve this by installing new fountains with a chiller, a filter, and a vertical filler. We are also expanding our idea by providing a dashboard for the grades to friendly competition for how many disposable water bottles can be saved, and reduce the carbon footprint.

How it Works:

Step 1:

Teachers can check if there is already a friendly competition going on, by scrolling down

Step 2:

If there is not, teachers can select a grade from both of the drop down menus and press the start button, which will begin a weeklong encouragment between the grades to see who can save the most plastic water bottles.

Step 3:

Tell your students to be ready to bring in their resuable water bottles!

Contest ends in:

6th Grade is in the lead followed by 8th and lastly 7th!
